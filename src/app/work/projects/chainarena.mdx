## Overview

**ChainArena** is a competitive **blockchain gaming platform** built for the next generation of esports.  
Players can join tournaments, form teams, and compete across their favorite titles to win **crypto payouts and NFT achievements**, all powered by **Solana** for speed, transparency, and fairness.  

---

## Features

- 🏆 **Win Crypto & NFTs** — Instant Solana payouts, on-chain trophies, and exclusive NFT rewards.  
- 🎮 **Any Game Supported** — Flexible tournament hosting for shooters, racing games, strategy, and more.  
- ⚡ **Powered by Solana** — Blazing-fast transactions, ultra-low fees, and provably fair results.  
- 👥 **Team Play** — Create squads or join existing teams to compete in group tournaments.  
- 📊 **Leaderboard & Rewards** — Track top players, team earnings, and win rates.  

---

## How It Works

1. **Connect Wallet** → Authenticate securely with Solana wallet.  
2. **Join Tournaments** → Browse upcoming events and register instantly.  
3. **Form Teams** → Team up and strategize for competitive advantage.  
4. **Win Rewards** → Claim crypto/NFTs directly in your wallet.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js + Tailwind  
- **Backend**: Node.js (Express) + Redis + PostgreSQL  
- **Blockchain**: Solana (wallet integration, on-chain payouts, NFT achievements)  
- **Infra**: Docker + Kubernetes  
- **Auth**: Wallet-based authentication (no passwords)
